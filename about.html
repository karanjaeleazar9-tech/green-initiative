About Us

Green Initiative was founded in 2015 with the goal of promoting environmental sustainability in local communities. We believe in hands-on action to make a tangible difference.

Our Mission

To educate, engage, and empower communities to take care of the environment through projects, workshops, and advocacy.

Our Vision

A world where communities live in harmony with nature and actively protect the environment for future generations.
